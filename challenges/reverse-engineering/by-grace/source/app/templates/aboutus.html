About MEMInc.

Founded in 1963, Magpie Electronic Machines Incorporated (better known as MEMI) was founded by people with a deep interest in data, both its creation and storage. From our earliest days we have pioneered ways to not only store the new data being created every day, but also to bring old data up to modern standards. Our Founder

With a bold vision of a future where man and machine work hand in hand, Magpie was founded by Dr. Eldon Tyrell in 1963. His futuristic vision of machines aiding in humanity's ascension to a new plane of evolution continues to drive the spirit of excellence and success for everything we do at Magpie!
